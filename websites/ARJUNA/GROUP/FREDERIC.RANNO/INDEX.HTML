Frédéric Ranno

Biography

Frédéric Ranno is a French engineer in Computing Science and Applied Mathematics (French M.Eng.) from the ENSIMAG, Grenoble, France (part of the INPG) in 1994. He received a Licence es Mathematics (French B.SC) from the University J. Fourier, Grenoble, France in 1992 while studying at the ENSIMAG. He also received a D.E.A. (French M.Sc. Research) in Computing Science from the INPG in 1994. He is currently a PhD student in the Department of Computing Science at Newcastle. His research interests include distributed computing, reliable systems, object-oriented computing and tools. His current work is in the area of reliable workflow systems.
