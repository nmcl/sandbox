Arjuna Group Funding

The Arjuna group in the past and currently receives funding from numerous different sources.

Current Funding

    Industrial

    • Nortel Technology (Northern Telecom) are sponsoring research into the construction of a reliable workflow systems.
    • GPT are sponsoring research into fault-tolerance in telecommunications systems.
    • Hewlett Packard (HP) are sponsoring research into dependable web services and disaster recovery in distributed systems.

    Academic

    • BROADCAST working group, sponsored by ESPRIT
    • C3DS (Control and Coordination of Complex Distributed Services), sponsored by ESPRIT

Past Funding

    Industrial

    • Integration of the Arjuna system in Openbase, sponsored by Prism Technologies, Newcastle upon Tyne, England.
    • Student registration system, sponsored by MITS, University of Newcastle upon Tyne, England.

    Academic

    • Realising Our Potential Award (ROPA) on building responsive and available information discovery and repository services, sponsored by EPSRC.
    • Basic Research On Advanced Distributed Computing: from Algorithms to SysTems (BROADCAST), sponsored by ESPRIT (Basic research project 6360)
    • Tools and architecture for building fault-tolerant applications, sponsored by SERC.
    • Design of high performance distributed fault tolerant systems, sponsored by SERC.
    • Fault tolerant microprocessor systems, sponsored by SERC.
    • Integrated Systems Architecture (ISA), sponsored by ESPRIT II. (Project 2267)
