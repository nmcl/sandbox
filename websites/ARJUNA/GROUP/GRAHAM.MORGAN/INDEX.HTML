Graham Morgan

Biography

Graham Morgan received his B.Sc. in Computing Science & Information Systems, from Salford University in 1994 and a M.Sc. in Computing Software & Systems Design from the University of Newcastle upon Tyne in 1995. He is currently a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computing Science at Newcastle. His research interests include distributed computing, reliable systems, CORBA programming and group communications. His recent work has been in the area of group communications and the design of CORBA services.
