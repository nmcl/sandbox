Jianrong Chen

Biography

Jianrong Chen comes from Huazhong University of Science and Technology, P. R of China. He is presently working at University of Newcastle upon Tyne as a visiting scholar.

Current Activities

Jianrong Chen's current research interests include: object-oriented distributed technology, mobile agents, workflow management systems and Java programming.
