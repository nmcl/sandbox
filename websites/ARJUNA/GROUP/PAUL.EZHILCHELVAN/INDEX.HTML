Dr. Paul Ezhilchelvan

Biography

Paul Devadoss Ezhilchelvan received his Ph.D. degree in computer science in 1989 from the university of Newcastle upon Tyne, England, for research work in agreement protocols and failure classification. He received the Bachelor of Engineering degree in 1981 from the University of Madras, India, and the Master of Engineering degree in 1983 from the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore. He joined the Department of Computing Science of the University of Newcastle upon Tyne in 1983 where he is currently a lecturer. His main research interests are in the areas of replicated processing, ordered and fault-tolerant multicast protocols, and group membership in synchronous and asynchronous distributed systems.
