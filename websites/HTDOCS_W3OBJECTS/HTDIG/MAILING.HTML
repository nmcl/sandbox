There is a mailing list for ht://Dig. Subscription messages can be sent to htdig-request@sdsu.edu. The message must contain a line like: subscribe username@hostname, where username@hostname is your preferred email address. Mailing list messages should be sent to htdig@contigo.com.

An archive of all ht://Dig mailing messages is available at http://www.eosys.com/mail-archives/htdig@sdsu.edu/.
